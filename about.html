My first big step into the realm of data science was when I learnt about regression in Econometrics back in King's Business School. I started to explore more, taking online courses and doing my own personal projects. I genuinely enjoyed exploring new concepts and figuring out a tough challenge/error.

When I realised I was excitedly explaining to my friends about this brilliant workaround in a particular algorithm and how cool it is, I knew then to aim for it as a career, to be a data scientist! (ngl, the title is also just too cool)
Hence, everyday I commit myself to consistently learn something new, no matter how small it might seems, whether that be about Cloud Infrastructure, Data Science, Machine Learning or matters of the economy.
